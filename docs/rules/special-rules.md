# Team special rules

Every team carries one or more **special rules**. Most are **league affiliations**
that gate which star players and inducements the team may hire; a few are
**mechanical** team rules. Star players (`playsFor`) and inducements
(`restrictedTo`) reference these by name. The same data is in `@blitz/data`
(`specialRules`).

## Leagues

| ----------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |

## Mechanical rules

| Rule                   | Effect                                                                                                                                                                                                   | Teams                                                     |
| ---------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| Brawlin' Brutes        | Players earn 3 SPP for causing a [[casualty]] (not 2), but only 2 SPP for a Touchdown (not 3).                                                                                                           | Black Orc, Dwarf, Khorne, Nurgle, Ogre, Orc               |
| Bribery and Corruption | Once per game, re-roll a 1 when Arguing the Call. Bribes cost 50,000 gp (limit 0-6) and a Biased Referee costs 80,000 gp.                                                                                | Black Orc, Dwarf, Goblin, Snotling, Underworld Denizens   |
| Favoured of Hashut     | The team's Chaos alignment (Hashut) — governs which Chaos star players it may hire.                                                                                                                      | Chaos Dwarf                                               |
| Favoured of Khorne     | The team's Chaos alignment (Khorne) — governs which Chaos star players it may hire.                                                                                                                      | Khorne, Norse                                             |
| Favoured of Nurgle     | The team's Chaos alignment (Nurgle) — governs Chaos star access and lets the team take the Plague Doctor inducement.                                                                                     | Nurgle                                                    |
| Low Cost Linemen       | In league play, Lineman hiring fees count as 0 gp toward Team Value; the team may take the Riotous Rookies inducement.                                                                                   | Ogre, Snotling                                            |
| Masters of Undeath     | Once per game, Raise the Dead — a slain opposition player of ST 4 or less rises as a free Zombie or Skeleton Lineman in your reserves (kept after the game). May take the Mortuary Assistant inducement. | Necromantic Horror, Shambling Undead, Tomb Kings, Vampire |
| Swarming               | At the start of each drive, set up D3 extra Lineman from reserves, beyond the usual eleven.                                                                                                              | Snotling                                                  |
| Team Captain           | Nominate a non-Big-Guy starter as captain: it gains [[pro]] for free, and whenever you use a team re-roll a natural 6 makes that re-roll free.                                                           | Human, Orc                                                |
