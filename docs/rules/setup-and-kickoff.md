# Setup, kick-off & weather

How a drive begins: setting up, kicking off, the kick-off event, and the weather.

## Team setup

- Both teams set up in their own half (up to 11 players each); the **kicking**
  team sets up first, then the **receiving** team.
- **At least 3** players must stand on the Line of Scrimmage (the centre field,
  adjacent to the line).
- **No more than 2** players may stand in each Wide Zone.
- Players not set up wait in the **Reserves box**. A team with **3 or fewer**
  players able to set up must put **all** of them on the Line of Scrimmage.

## Coin toss

A roll-off; the winner chooses whether to kick or receive.

## The kick

- The kicking team nominates a **kicker** — **not** on the Line of Scrimmage and
  **not** in a Wide Zone — and places the ball in any square of the **opponent's**
  half.
- The ball **deviates D6 squares** in a random direction to the square it lands
  in.
- A **kick-off event** is rolled (2D6) and resolved; **only then** does the ball
  come down — caught if it lands on a player, bouncing on an empty square.
- **Touchback** — if the ball leaves the pitch, or crosses back over the Line of
  Scrimmage into the kicking team's half, then **after the event** the receiving
  coach gives it to a standing player (or an empty square) in their own half.

## Kick-off event table (2D6)

| 2D6 | Event              | Effect                                                                             |
| --- | ------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2   | Get the Ref        | each team gains a free Bribe                                                       |
| 3   | Time-out!          | both turn markers shift one space                                                  |
| 4   | Solid Defence      | the kicking team may re-set up to D3+3 open players                                |
| 5   | High Kick          | one open receiving player may be placed under the landing square                   |
| 6   | Cheering Fans      | roll D6 + Cheerleaders; the winner gets a +1 assist on their first block next turn |
| 7   | Brilliant Coaching | roll D6 + Assistant Coaches; the winner gains a free team re-roll for the drive    |
| 8   | Changing Weather   | roll on the Weather table again                                                    |
| 9   | Quick Snap!        | the receiving team may move up to D3+3 open players one square each                |
| 10  | Charge!            | the kicking team may give up to D3+3 open players a free Move / Blitz / Throw      |
| 11  | Dodgy Snack        | random players risk a stat penalty for the drive                                   |
| 12  | Pitch Invasion     | both roll D6 + Fan Factor; the lower roll has D3 players knocked Prone and Stunned |

## Weather table (2D6)

| 2D6  | Weather            | Effect                                                        |
| ---- | ------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 2    | Sweltering Heat    | at each drive's end, both coaches send D3 players to Reserves |
| 3    | Very Sunny         | −1 to Passing tests                                           |
| 4–10 | Perfect Conditions | no effect                                                     |
| 11   | Pouring Rain       | −1 to pick up, catch or intercept                             |
| 12   | Blizzard           | −1 to Rush; only Quick and Short passes allowed               |
